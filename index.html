About World Heritage Sites

World Heritage sites are a means to preserve the natural and cultural heritage of humanity, in a very utopian Star Trek sort of way. However, World Heritage sites are also used as political tools, often involving disputed areas and conflicts with indigenous people. World Heritage sites are culturally legitimising, allowing states to reinforce their claims over the past. So more like the way Cardassia attempted legitimize their claim on Bajor by possessing the Orbs of the Prophets, important Bajoran cultural objects. Other times the size and power of a country influences the ability to have a location declared a World Heritage site.
